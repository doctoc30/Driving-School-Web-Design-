Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up a chatbot?

Typically, we can have a basic chatbot implemented on your website within 3-5 business days. More complex implementations with custom integrations may take 2-3 weeks.

Do I need technical knowledge to use the chatbot?

No technical knowledge is required. We provide an easy-to-use dashboard where you can train your chatbot, view conversations, and update responses without any coding.

Can the chatbot integrate with my CRM?

Yes, our Business and Enterprise plans include CRM integration with popular platforms like Salesforce, HubSpot, and Zoho. We can also create custom integrations for specific needs.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support based on your plan. This includes setup assistance, training for your team, and ongoing maintenance. Our support team is available via email and chat during business hours.
