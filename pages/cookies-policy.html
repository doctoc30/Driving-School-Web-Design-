Cookies Policy

Last updated: 5 September 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how Driving School Web Design ("we", "our", "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. It also explains your rights and choices regarding cookies, in accordance with UK GDPR and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR).

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies help the website to function correctly, provide a better user experience, and allow us to collect information about how the site is used.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze website usage. Cookies help us to:

  • Ensure that the website works properly and efficiently
  • Remember your preferences, such as language and region
  • Measure how you use the site so we can improve it
  • Provide personalized content or marketing (where applicable)

Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website to function. Without these, features like forms and login sessions may not work.
  • Performance / Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website, which pages are most popular, and how users navigate the site. We use anonymized data only.
  • Functionality / Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices (like preferred language or region) to improve your browsing experience.
  • Targeting / Advertising Cookies: Track browsing habits to deliver relevant advertisements. These are typically set by third-party advertising networks.

Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services such as analytics providers or social media platforms. We do not control these cookies, and their use is subject to the respective third-party privacy policies. Examples include:

  • Google Analytics – for anonymized traffic analysis
  • Social media sharing buttons – for content sharing and engagement

Your consent

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner will appear. By clicking “Accept All,” you consent to the use of all cookies. You can also adjust your preferences or reject non-essential cookies by clicking “Manage Preferences.”

Managing and deleting cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block or restrict cookies
  • Clear all cookies when closing the browser

Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

Data protection and privacy

Any data collected via cookies is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We do not share personal data collected through cookies with unauthorized third parties.

Changes to this Cookies Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law or how we use cookies. The latest version will always be available on this page, and the “last updated” date will indicate when it was most recently revised.
